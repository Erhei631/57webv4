Industries Health

Device-connected health platforms that clear the bar.

We build digital health, remote patient monitoring, and clinical workflow software where reliability, compliance, and clinician trust are not optional. The patterns below recur across the health platforms we deliver.

Talk to our team How we deliver
✸ The problem

In health software, trust is the product.

Regulated health work has to be reliable, compliant, and auditable from the first day, not retrofitted. These are the patterns we bring to every health platform, regardless of the specific condition or care setting.

✸ Patterns we bring

Repeatable across health platforms, not one-off.

Device-connected monitoring

Bluetooth and medical-device integration with device-to-app-to-cloud pipelines, auto-capturing most measurements so clinicians enter less by hand.

Multi-surface, role-based platforms

A patient or clinician mobile app, a provider web surface, and practice and admin layers on one data model, with access controls that wall off who can see clinical data.

Compliance engineered in

HIPAA, GDPR, NIS-2, and data-residency designed into the infrastructure, with field-level encryption, audit logs, and readiness for annual and medical-device audits.

Clinical AI with enforced guardrails

Reflections, mood and condition models, and assessments constrained by clinical guidelines, with crisis detection and escalation enforced in the system rather than the prompt.

EHR and system-of-record integration

Integrate with national or hospital EHRs as the source of truth, importing patients and writing measurements back so clinicians never enter data twice.

Alerting and time-saving workflow

Threshold-based alerts and escalation handoffs, plus care-group scoping and auto-fill that cut the time a clinician spends per patient.
