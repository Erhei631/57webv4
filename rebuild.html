Replace legacy systems and partial-fit SaaS.

We rebuild bloated, business-critical custom systems and the partial-fit SaaS that companies have outgrown. ✸ The shift

When rebuilding becomes viable, tailored beats partial-fit.

Work that was previously too risky and too slow to touch becomes practical: tailored systems for businesses that had settled for partial-fit SaaS, and ground-up rebuilds of legacy enterprise systems, held to the same quality bar as a new build.

✸ How it works

Recover, rebuild, migrate.

The same machinery that recovers what the old system actually does then rebuilds it tailored — and migrates your data into it.

Excavator

Recovers requirements, behaviors, and the data model from the running system — surfacing what it actually does before anything is rebuilt.

How we deliver

Users as oracle
