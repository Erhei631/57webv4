✸ The model

Fixed fee. Committed cadence. Visible gates.

You buy an outcome on a predictable cadence, not a timesheet. The fee is anchored to capacity, the release rhythm is ours to hold, and quality is tracked against metrics you can see.

Fixed monthly fee

Anchored to pod capacity, not hours billed. PTO, public holidays, and sprint fluctuation are absorbed. No monthly recompute.

Cadence we own

One release per sprint. We commit the delivery; you commit timely requirements. Both sides are visible on a shared dashboard.

Quality gates, not hour reports

Acceptance, crash-free rate, and equivalent metrics calibrated to your baseline. That is how you keep oversight without managing our time.
