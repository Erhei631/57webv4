✸ Why it is different

Built for a services firm, not a developer's IDE.

Operating a harness inside a services firm with contractual delivery accountability changes the design. Five constraints follow from that, and they shape the whole system.

Specifications become reviewable contracts that clients approve before any code is generated.

Failures surface in stakeholder-readable form, not buried in logs only an engineer can parse.

Economics are dominated by labor rather than compute, which changes what is worth automating.

Audit traceability is first-class, recorded as a matter of course rather than reconstructed after the fact.

The team is a design parameter, with senior people placed where judgment matters most.
