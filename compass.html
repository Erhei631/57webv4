✸ Why it matters

The benefit of work that is already solved.

You get the benefit of work that is already solved. Instead of a team discovering the right architecture on your time and your budget, the approaches that have worked, and the mistakes that have not, are applied from the start. Your project ramps faster and ships at a higher quality bar.

Compass spans two kinds of knowledge: horizontal engineering practice across frontend, backend, mobile, testing, and product, and vertical best practices for your domain in financial services, health, legal, and consumer. Both are used to set the depth of your specification and to guide the build, so the decisions that are expensive to get wrong are made well, and made early.

Your intellectual property stays yours. Compass is built from our own engineering craft, not from client code or data. What your team builds with us belongs to you. What we bring is the reusable patterns and hard-won lessons that make a senior team senior, available to your project from day one.
