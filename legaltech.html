Industries LegalTech

Legal and IP systems, from document AI to scale.

We build legal and intellectual-property AI, the operational systems firms run on, and the backend that carries a legal-AI product from MVP to scale. The patterns below recur across the legal platforms we deliver.

Talk to our team How we deliver
✸ The problem

Most legal software is a 70% fit, and accuracy has to match an expert.

Firms settle for tools that almost work, and in legal and IP work an answer that is merely plausible is worthless. The bar is expert-level accuracy and a system that scales once real users arrive.

✸ Patterns we bring

Repeatable across legal and IP platforms, not one-off.

Domain-tuned document and search AI

Extraction, classification, and search over large legal and patent corpora, using fine-tuned models, embeddings, prompt engineering, and vector databases rather than off-the-shelf output.

Accuracy benchmarked to experts

Quality measured against expert ground truth on precision, recall, and ranking, so results match what examiners and attorneys actually expect.

Retrieval and knowledge layers

Reasoning over a firm's own corpus with provenance and review built in, so every answer can be traced to its source.

Backend that scales post-MVP

Taking a launched legal-AI product and building the backend infrastructure to scale reliably to many more users.

IP rights infrastructure, including on-chain

Systems for managing intellectual-property rights, including registering and managing IP on blockchain.

Firm operations

Matter management, intake, and workflow systems tailored to how a firm actually works, replacing partial-fit SaaS.

✸ How we engage

Lead with the solution, pair with the offer.

LegalTech splits across build and rebuild. We build new document and IP AI products, scale the ones already launched, and rebuild partial-fit SaaS into tailored systems.
