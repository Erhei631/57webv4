The challenge

In the early pre-seed stage, the Supio founders had a powerful vision for LegalAI but needed to validate it across various verticals. Hiring top-tier product and engineering talent at this stage is notoriously slow.

To find product-market fit, the founders needed the capacity to ship multiple MVPs, test hypotheses, and iterate on learnings immediately without the lead time of building an internal team from scratch.

The solution

57Blocks served as Supio's elastic product and engineering partner, evolving our role to match their growth stages:

Pre-seedThe iteration engine

We provided an embedded team of engineers and designers that acted as an extension of the founders. This allowed Supio to rapidly build and ship demos and MVPs across different legal verticals, giving the founders the freedom to test ideas and pivot based on real-time insights.

Seed stageFeature velocity & scale

As Supio gained traction, we scaled the engineering team to accelerate the delivery of critical product components. While their internal team focused on core IP, we built out essential features and supported the underlying infrastructure required to ensure high-quality model performance.

Series A & beyondElastic scaling

As Supio grew, we continued to scale alongside their core teams, providing the engineering capacity needed to meet increasing market demand without slowing down development.

The impact

By leveraging 57Blocks, Supio bypassed the early-stage talent bottleneck. They successfully navigated the “search” phase of the startup lifecycle, iterating until they found their core value proposition and seamlessly transitioned into the “execute” phase with a ready-made, high-quality engineering team. This partnership enabled them to secure product-market fit and raise capital through Series X.
